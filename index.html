Featured Recipe - Instant Lasagna

A simple and delicious recipe for lasgna using common ingredients that you probably already have in your cupboard or refrigerator, that is so quick and easy to make
that we like to call it Instant Lasagna!.

Ingredients Needed

Make sure you have the following on hand before you begin.

  • 20 oz ravioli, frozen
  • 3 cups meat sauce or jarred sauce
  • ½ cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella (≅1 bag)
  • parmesan cheese for sprinkling
  • 1 tsp. pepper
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder

Instructions

Follow the below instructions carefully for a delicious meal. Take your time and you will be rewarded.

  1. Cook raviolis through, according to the directions on the package.
  2. While they are cooking, heat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Mix the meat sauce with the ricotta so it is combined.
  4. Spray an oven-safe dish with cooking spray
  5. Put about ½ cup of the sauce on the bottom of the dish and spread it out.
  6. Layer on a single layer of ravioli.
  7. Top with some mozzarella cheese, a sprinkle of parmesan cheese and another ½ cup of sauce.
  8. Keep doing this until you have layered all the raviolis.
  9. Try to only use one-half of the mozzarella cheese while layering.
  10. Place the remaining mozzarella on top.
  11. Cook this for about 25 minutes or until bubbly, no need to put foil on top.

Now all that is left for you to do is serve and enjoy.
